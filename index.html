🔥 Key Features
🌀

Hybrid IR + EQ

Integrates impulse response convolution with a full 20‑band parametric EQ in series/parallel. Real‑time spectrum analyzer for instant feedback.

🧠

Adaptive Noise Shaper

9th‑order noise shaper with CMA‑ES continuous coefficient learning. Automatically reduces quantization distortion while preserving transparency during downsampling.

⚡

Low Latency & Linear Phase

Choose between IIR (ultra‑low latency) and Linear Phase FIR modes. Optimal latency for live performance or mixing.

🎚️

Analog‑style Filters

HCF/LCF selectable from Sharp, Natural, or Soft. EQ bands with Q from 0.1 to 12.0, ±24 dB gain.

📀

Professional I/O

Supports ASIO, WASAPI, DirectSound. Up to 768 kHz / 32‑bit floating point processing. Headroom adjustment and output makeup gain.

🔬

Tailorable IR Tail

"Layer Tail Contouring" for independent control of high‑frequency tail time. Fine‑tune reverb character with mix start frequency and tail strength.
